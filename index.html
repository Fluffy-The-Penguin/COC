How to Use This Tracker

1

Find Your Player Tag

Open Clash of Clans, go to your profile, and copy your player tag (it starts with #).

2

Enter Your Tag

Enter your player tag in the input field above (include the # symbol).

3

Fetch Your Data

Click "Fetch Village Data" to load your statistics from the Clash of Clans API.

4

Explore Your Stats

View your village stats, achievements, clan info, and more in the dashboard.

No API Key Needed: The server-side API key is already configured. Just enter your player tag!

Privacy: Your player tag is only used to fetch data from the official Clash of Clans API.
